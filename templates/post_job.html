Tips for Better Job Posts

✅ Do:
  • Use specific job titles
  • List required skills clearly
  • Describe company culture
  • Include salary range if possible
  • Be specific about responsibilities
❌ Don't:
  • Use vague descriptions
  • Forget to list key skills
  • Use jargon excessively
  • Make the description too long
  • Forget location/remote details
